Stuffing Meatloaf

This meatloaf with stuffing mix is easy to make for a super savory meatloaf!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • ¾ cup chicken-flavored bread stuffing mix (such as Kraft® Stove Top®)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.

Step 2

Mix beef, onion, stuffing mix, and egg by hand in a bowl. Press beef mixture into the prepared pan.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 35 to 40 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Step 4

Top meatloaf with shredded mozzarella cheese and let rest while cheese melts, about 10 minutes.

Tips

This meatloaf can also be made in 4 mini loaf pans and bakeed for 25 to 30 minutes.
